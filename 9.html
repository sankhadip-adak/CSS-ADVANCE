Apple Inc

It was incorporated as Apple Computer, Inc. in January 1977, and sales of its computers, including the Apple II , saw significant momentum and revenue growth for the company. "Inc." is the abbreviation for incorporated. A corporation is a separate legal entity from the person or people forming it.

Apple Inc., formerly Apple Computer, Inc., American manufacturer of personal computers, smartphones, tablet computers, computer peripherals, and computer software and one of the most recognizable brands in the world.

Apple Inc (Apple) designs, manufactures, and markets smartphones, tablets, personal computers, and wearable devices. The company offers software applications and related services, accessories, and third-party digital content.
